Public Intimacies

\ \

Public Intimacies was a collaboratively curated special events programme hosted at the Institute of Contemporary Arts (ICA) in London for the closing weekend of the London Short Film Festival 2020.

\ \

The programme was an investigation into women\'92s auto-ethnographic practices, focusing on the video diary form. I conceptualised, researched and produced the events with Jenna Roberts.

\ \

The three-day programme featured:

\ \
    \
  • Public Intimacies: Anne Charlotte Robinson\'92s Five Year Diary // Short films programmed by Thomas Grimshaw and Jenna Roberts, with an introduction by Daniella Shrier (Another Gaze).
    • \
  • Public Intimacies: Talking to Myself // Short films programmed by Miranda Mungai and Jenna Roberts.
    • \
  • Public Intimacies: Profession: Documentarist // Feature film programmed by Miranda Mungai.
    • \
  • Public Intimacies: A Programme of Films by Alia Syed // Short films programmed by Jemma Desai.
    • \
  • Public Intimacies: Auto-ethnography as Refusal // Presentation by Jemma Desai and Rabz Lansiquot.
    • \

With Warmth and Ecstatic Solidarity

\ \

With Warmth and Ecstatic Solidarity was a virtual special events programme of short documentaries presented at the London Short Film Festival 2021.

\ \

The programme looked at filmmaking from various diasporas and the potential for international solidarity. I moderated a conversation with filmmakers Bircan Birol and Suneil Sanzgiri after the event.
